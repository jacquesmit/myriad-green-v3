Only once we have the exact location. We mark neatly and provide alternatives when possible.

Most homes are completed within 2–3 hours. Large estates or multiple leaks may take longer.

Do you assist with insurance claims?

Yes. Our reports include photographic evidence, readings, and a narrative log.

Can you detect leaks under paving or pools?

We use hydrophones and tracer gas to detect under paved areas, pools, and water features.
