FAQs

Can you work with my existing controller?

Yes. We integrate Hunter, Rain Bird, Gardena, and Hydrawise controllers or upgrade to cloud-connected units when needed.

Do you trench across finished paving?

We core drill or use existing sleeves wherever possible, and re-instate paving with matching materials.

How do you prevent overwatering?

Moisture sensors, flow meters, and weather data adjust run times automatically.

Can you integrate borehole or rainwater supply?

Absolutely. We balance municipal, borehole, and rainwater sources with filtration and pump controls.
